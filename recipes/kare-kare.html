Kare-Kare

This perennial Filipino favorite usually starts with a base of oxtails, beef stew cuts, pork hocks or tripe. Traditionally, the choice meat is simmered for hours to desired tenderness and and along with a variety of vegetables such as banana heart, long beans and eggplant, it's pulled together into a stew with ground peanuts for flavor, toasted ground rice for thickening and annatto for coloring.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds oxtail cut into serving size
  • water
  • 1 banana heart
  • 2 large eggplant ends trimmed and cut into 1-inch thick
  • 4 to 5 cloves garlic peeled and minced
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • shrimp paste
